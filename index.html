Defiant-Class Warship

The new generation of star ships ensure Federation security through rapid response to threat forces. Funding for the Defiant Class Project was funded by the Advanced Starship Design Bureau.

Built with Pride at the Antares Fleet Yard, Antares IV

Where the magic happens!

A Defiant-class starship is a heavily armored, limited-role Starfleet vessel developed at the Antares Fleet Yards in response to the Borg threat to the worlds of the Alpha and Beta Quadrants.

  • Classification: Escort (Defensive/Patrol/Reconnaissance)
  • Accommodation: 40 Officers and Crew
  • Length: 119.5 meters
  • Width: 90.3 meters
  • Height: 25.5 meters
  • Mass: 355,000 metric tonnes
  • Cargo Capacity: 10,477 metric tonnes
  • Hull: Ablative armor overlaying a duranium/tritanium composite hull, augmented by synthetic castrodium alloy structural members
  • Number of Decks: 4
  • Cloaking Tech: Optional upgrade to a Romulan Cloaking Device (see the Treaty of Algeron for details)
  • Holodeck: None, but see near by Quarks for a Holosuite

Overgunned and Overpowered

She is Battle Ready

The ship was designed specifically for battle, featuring innovative pulse-phaser cannons and quantum torpedo armaments, in addition to photon torpedoes, standard phasers and a high-capacity deflector shield system. Another asset is its ablative armour, enabling the ship to sustain multiple hits from enemy weapons, even with the shields inoperable, with minimal damage.

Borg Threat

Resist this. The Defiant development began around 2366 in response to the Borg threat to the Federation. We call her an " escort vessel" officially, but the Defiant is nothing less than Starfleet's first design of warship, designed to combat the Borg. They will be shaking in their little Borg booties. The vessel features minimal equipment for scientific research and was not designed to accommodate families

The Future

Designed to be fast and highly maneuverable with powerful weaponry the Defiant will serve for years to come. Should an interstellar state and military superpower from the Gamma Quadrant happened to start a war with the federation she wounldn't go down without a fight.
